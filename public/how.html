How WasteLink Works

We bridge communities, NGOs, and staff — transforming waste problems into clean, green actions for a sustainable future.

1. Report Waste

Citizens upload photos of waste spots using our Report Page. Each submission includes a location and brief description.

2. Staff Review

Our staff verifies the uploaded reports, ensuring location accuracy and categorizing the type of waste for effective action.

3. NGO Assignment

The verified report is assigned to the nearest registered NGO capable of handling the cleanup task efficiently.

4. Cleanup in Action

NGOs conduct cleanups, manage collected waste responsibly, and upload evidence such as images or progress updates.

5. Track & Celebrate

The completed tasks are displayed publicly, motivating communities to participate and celebrate their environmental impact.
